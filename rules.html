Conference Rules

  • Participants must remain respectful and cooperative at all times.
  • Phones should remain silent during committee and GA sessions.
  • Delegates must follow the instructions of the chairs and organizers.
  • Formal or semi-formal attire is required during all official sessions.
  • Debate must follow parliamentary procedure and speaking order.
  • Any disruptive behavior may result in removal from the session.
  • Delegates are expected to participate actively and constructively.
