About PEYPC

A step towards full equality or an infringement on national sovereignty?
The Pefki European Youth Parliament Conference (PEYPC) is a student‑driven simulation of the European Parliament, hosted at Pefki Municipal Theatre on 13/3/2026.

Our goal is to provide students with the opportunity to:
  • Engage in structured debate
  • Develop critical thinking and diplomacy skills
  • Collaborate with peers on European policy issues
  • Experience the procedures of parliamentary committees
PEYPC is organized by students, for students — promoting leadership, teamwork, and civic awareness.
